How to Interact with Mario

Press any of the arrow keys to move him.

Press the space bar to make him jump.

Click on him to hear his greeting.

Press and hold down the "s" key to make him invincible!

- OR -

Transport him to a new level!

  1. Press "1" to send him to the Field Level
  2. Press "2" to send him to the Desert Level
  3. Press "3" to send him to the Coast Level

Press the "h" key to hide/show these instructions
